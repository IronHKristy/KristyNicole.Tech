About Me

During my second year of college, while studying graphic design, I took a Visual Basic class as an elective to get out of math. Thus, began my love affair for computer programming. I switched majors to a computer information systems degree but quickly realized that I was pursuing a degree that had nothing to do with what I wanted to do. That’s when I heard of “coding bootcamps.” I did my research, chose a highly spoken of school, IronHack, and spent 10 weeks learning so much more than I could ever imagine would be possible in such a short time. I know my journey has just begun and I can’t wait to see what else is possible.
